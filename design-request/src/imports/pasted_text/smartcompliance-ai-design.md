Design a complete SaaS product UI/UX for an enterprise fintech platform called “SmartCompliance AI” — an AI-powered AML/KYC compliance intelligence system used by banks, fintech companies, and payment platforms.

Create a modern, premium, futuristic enterprise dashboard UI with glassmorphism elements, AI-inspired visuals, and data-rich analytics components.

The design should look like a next-generation fintech intelligence platform similar to Stripe Radar, Chainalysis, or ComplyAdvantage but more modern and intuitive.

Use:

• Glassmorphism cards
• Blurred background panels
• Gradient AI colors (blue, purple, cyan)
• subtle glowing elements
• modern fintech typography
• smooth micro interactions
• professional enterprise layout
• high readability for data-heavy screens

Buttons, cards, and panels should use glass effects with subtle borders, blur, and glow.

Add realistic illustrations, AI graphics, charts, and fintech themed images.
